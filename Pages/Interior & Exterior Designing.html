Interior & Exterior Designing

What is Interior & Exterior Design ?

Interior design is the process of designing the interior of a home or other structure. This can involve anything from choosing paint colors and furniture to deciding on the layout of a room. Exterior design is the process of designing the exterior of a home or other structure. People are shifting to new trends and experimenting with new ways as technology develops and changes. Scope:

With the changing lifestyle and rapid urbanization, the scope of Interior Design has grown tremendously. Market trends are showing remarkable growth in demand for Interior designers. Many people rely on professionals to design homes and other places to achieve a comfortable and happy life. Students pursuing interior design can find many career opportunities, working with architects, exhibition designers, and engineers.

Designs are created in response to and coordinated with the building shell and acknowledge the physical location and social context of the project. Designs must adhere to code and regulatory requirements, and encourage the principles of environmental feasibility.
